About Food Circle

Delicious meals delivered fast, fresh, and at your convenience.

Our Story

Founded in Pune in 2022, Food Circle was born out of the simple desire to make great food accessible to everyone. Whether you're craving a spicy biryani, a crispy dosa, or a fresh cold coffee, we’ve got something for every food lover.

Our mission is to bring delicious, hygienic, and affordable meals right to your doorstep — every time, on time.
